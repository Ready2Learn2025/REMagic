About Robert Ellams

Based in Tameside between Manchester, Oldham and Huddersfield, Robert’s unique blend of astonishing close‑up magic and friendly comedic banter is perfect for weddings, birthdays, corporate dinners, charity fundraisers, and more. He tailors each performance—from formal corporate functions to intimate home gatherings—to your event’s style and duration.
