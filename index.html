About Robert Ellams

Based in Tameside in between Manchester, Oldham and Huddersfield, he is within easy reach of Yorkshire, Lancashire and Derbyshire and willing to travel anywhere in the country. Robert’s unique blend of astonishing magic with friendly comedic banter is a must for any events from Weddings, Birthdays, Corporate Dinners to Charity Fundraisers. From formal ‘corporate’ to the more informal ‘small gatherings’, large hospitality suites to the host or hostesses home. Robert can arrange the appropriate cards, coins and other routines to suit your needs and for the length of time you want him.
