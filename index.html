About Robert Ellams

Based in Tameside between Manchester, Oldham and Huddersfield, Robert travels nationwide. His unique blend of astonishing magic and friendly comedic banter elevates events from weddings and birthdays to corporate dinners and charity fundraisers.

With over a decade of experience, Robert specializes in close-up magic that will leave your guests amazed and talking about your event for years to come.
